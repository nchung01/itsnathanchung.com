About Me

I’m a Computer Engineering student from Guelph studying at the University of Guelph. What really intrigues me about technology is how far it has come—from a time when we had no technology at all to creating powerful devices capable of performing countless functions. It's incredible that a single device offers features such as communicating with people from across the world and providing forms of entertainment, like video games and YouTube. I’m interested in understanding what makes technology capable of performing such tasks. As a visual learner who enjoys building things, I find that coding and using CAD tools, such as SolidWorks, allow me to bring my ideas to life. What truly fascinates me is AI and its ability to take over tasks solely done by humans and shape the future. Outside of engineering, I enjoy spending time playing video games and with my family as well as traveling with them (Japan is on my bucket list). One of my personal goals is bettering myself, so I enjoy working out and trying to learn new life skills. When I think about technology, it's more than just coding and circuits. To me, it's about making our lives better and easier by creating tools.
